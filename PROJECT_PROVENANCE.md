## Project Purpose

MirageSentinel_Deception_Lab™ is a cyber deception and adversary-behavior intelligence lab built for portfolio, academic, and professional demonstration.

The project includes a Next.js analyst dashboard, Node/Express API, MongoDB-backed telemetry models, WebSocket event streaming, MITRE ATT&CK-style mapping, RL/deception decision panels, simulation controls, and deployment-ready Docker/Render configuration.
